Pause

A calming breath before you open distracting apps — so you open with intention, not reflex.

Download for Android

Free & open-source · no account · no tracking · no subscription

What it does

When you open an app you've chosen to pause, Pause covers the screen with a slow breathing circle and a short timed pause, and shows how many times you've opened that app today. Only then can you continue — or change your mind. It's gentle friction, not a hard block: enough to turn an automatic tap into a deliberate choice.

Features

Breathing pause

A slow, calming animation instead of an instant open.

Your apps, your rules

Pick exactly which apps get a pause.

Open count

See how often you've reached for an app in the last 24 hours.

Adjustable

Set the pause length, your own message, and hide the countdown.

Quiet stats

Track pauses, opens, cancels, and time spent breathing.

Private by design

Everything stays on your phone. No accounts, no analytics.

Set up

  1. Download the latest APK and open it on your phone (allow installs from unknown sources if asked).
  2. Open Pause and turn on accessibility access.
  3. Set your pause length and message, then choose the apps to pause.
